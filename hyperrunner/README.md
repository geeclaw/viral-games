# CUBE RUSH - Hypercasual Runner

## Concept
A fast-paced hypercasual runner where you tap left/right to switch lanes. Your cube leaves a colorful stack trail behind. Avoid obstacles, collect power-ups, survive as long as possible.

## Why It Will Go Viral

### 1. One-Tap Mechanic
- Extremely simple controls - anyone can play
- "Just one more try" addiction loop
- Perfect for short sessions (waiting in line, etc.)

### 2. Visual Satisfaction
- Satisfying cube stacking visuals
- Neon color scheme pops on screen
- Trail grows longer = more impressive
- Screen shake and flash effects

### 3. Clippable Content
- High score attempts are naturally dramatic
- Near-miss moments are thrilling to watch
- Perfect for TikTok/YouTube Shorts
- "Watch me beat my high score" videos

### 4. Competitive Hook
- Leaderboard drives competition
- Shareable high scores
- Simple enough to challenge friends

## Target Audience
- Casual mobile gamers
- TikTok/Shorts viewers (13-34)
- Anyone who loves "easy to learn, hard to master"

## Monetization Potential
- Rewarded ads for continue (high conversion)
- Skins/custom cubes
- Remove ads

## Similar Success Stories
- Subway Surfers (billions of downloads)
- Temple Run
- Helix Jump
